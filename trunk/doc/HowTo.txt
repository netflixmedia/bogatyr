Installation

1) Just download the newest Bogatyr JAR library bogatyr-*.jar from the project home and add it to your project.

2) After that, you can access the package over import ch.orwell.bogatyr.*.

3) Extract and copy the two folders to your workspace or import it in your IDE:

    * cfg 

    * res 

4) Move cfg/ch/orwell/bogatyr/standard.properties to cfg/yourapp/standard.properties file and enter the correct path settings

5) To run your app, enter this on your command prompt:

    * java -jar /lib/bogatyr-*.jar ch.orwell.yourapp.YourApp ./cfg/ch/orwell/yourapp/standard.properties 

6) Enjoy... 

7) ...if not, check your classpath settings and your java installation.
